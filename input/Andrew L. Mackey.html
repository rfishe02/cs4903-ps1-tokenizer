Summer 2019 Special Topics/Advanced Courses

  • CS 4903 - Text Mining: Text is fairly ubiquitous due its dominance on the Internet. The usefulness of information that can be extracted from text can be found in a variety of areas, including social networking, sentiment analysis, business intelligence, etc. The goal of this course is to provide the tools and techniques to extract knowledge from documents and facilitate the search and retrieval of data. In addition, students will have an opportunity to gain experience in building a modern search engine on large datasets. Prerequisites: Data Structures or have instructor approval.
